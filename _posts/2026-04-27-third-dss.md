On March 25, 2026, Digital Scholarship and Initiatives and the Center for Excellence in the Arts and Humanities (CEAH), in collaboration with partners at Grinnell College and the University of Iowa, hosted their third annual Digital Scholarship Symposium in the library's Catalyst space.

This year's theme, "Expanding Possibilities: Digital Scholarship for Communities, Classrooms, and Creative Futures," invited students, faculty, and staff from institutions across Iowa to explore how digital scholarship bolsters digital literacy and opens research to broader audiences. To learn more about the symposium, visit the [event site](https://instr.iastate.libguides.com/DSSymposium){:target="_blank" rel="noopener"}.
